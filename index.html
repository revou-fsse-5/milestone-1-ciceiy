Programs Offered

Source: CIA (Culinary Institute of America)

Culinary Arts

The Culinary Arts program at Gourmet Academy offers a comprehensive education in culinary techniques, food preparation, and kitchen management. Students gain hands-on experience in various culinary disciplines, from classical to contemporary cuisines.

Pastry & Baking Arts

Our Pastry & Baking Arts program provides an in-depth study of pastry techniques, dessert preparation, and bakery management. Students learn to create everything from breads to confections with precision and creativity.

Wine & Beverage Studies

The Wine & Beverage Studies program focuses on the intricacies of wine production, beverage management, and pairing techniques. This program equips students with the knowledge to become sommeliers or beverage directors.

Culinary Science

The Culinary Science program merges the art of cooking with scientific principles. Students explore food safety, nutrition, and innovative cooking techniques, preparing them for careers in research and development within the food industry.

Top Instructors

Meet 5 of our talented and experienced instructors who make Gourmet Academy a premier culinary school.

Chef Frank Vollkommer

A Certified Master Pastry Chef with over 30 years of experience, Director of Culinary Industry Development at Auguste Escoffier School, trained at New England Culinary Institute.

Chef Emily Luchetti

Celebrated pastry chef with over 30 years in the industry, Executive Pastry Chef at Waterbar and Farallon, inducted into the James Beard Foundation Who’s Who of Food & Beverage.

Chef David Chang

Renowned chef and founder of Momofuku, with over 20 years of experience, known for innovative cooking and earning Michelin stars.

Chef Dominique Crenn

The first female chef in the US to earn three Michelin stars, owner and Executive Chef at Atelier Crenn, with over 30 years of experience and a focus on artistic cuisine.

Chef Arnold Poernomo

An Indonesian-Australian chef known for his creative dishes and TV appearances. He rose to fame as a judge on "MasterChef Indonesia" and owns several successful restaurants.
